Scheduling

Pinch-to-zoom Gantt, right on your phone.

Build schedules with phases, dependencies, and milestones — no laptop required. Critical path auto-calculates. When approved change orders add days, the schedule shifts automatically.

  • Tap to edit duration, drag to reschedule
  • Critical path highlighted in red
  • AI risk forecast flags weather-sensitive tasks
  • Share a read-only view with the owner via portal

Daily Field Reports

Voice-in, save to project, send later.

Talk through the day and the app fills out the report — weather, crews, materials, issues, and incidents. Save it to the project first, send to the owner when you're ready. Photos automatically sync to the project gallery.

  • Voice-to-report transcription with AI parsing
  • Auto-fetched weather from project location
  • Photo gallery synced across project + DFR
  • OSHA-recordable incident workflow built in

AIA Pay Applications

G702 and G703, one tap away.

Progress billing that actually looks like what the architect expects. Retainage, stored materials, and prior billings all calculate correctly. Send as PDF or push straight to the portal for owner signature.

  • Auto-filled from your schedule of values
  • Retainage and stored materials handled
  • Exports to PDF with the real AIA form layout
  • Owner can approve from the client portal

Bid Discovery

Federal opportunities in your pocket.

SAM.gov federal contract opportunities refreshed every few hours. Filter by NAICS, location, set-aside, and dollar range. One tap to save, add to your bid pipeline, or open in SAM directly.

  • SAM.gov federal contracts refreshed daily
  • NAICS-code and keyword filters
  • Set-aside filters (8(a), WOSB, HUBZone, SDVOSB)
  • Saved searches with push alerts
