Common Questions

How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email. We'll send you a secure reset link.

Is my project data secure?

Yes. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest, stored on SOC 2–compliant infrastructure. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I export my data?

Yes. From Settings → Data Export, you can download all of your projects, estimates, invoices, and reports as a portable file. We believe your data should always be yours.

How does the client portal work?

From any project, tap "Share with Client" to generate a secure link. Your client can view the schedule, progress, daily reports, and approved documents without needing an account.

Does MAGE ID work offline?

Most day-to-day features (creating daily reports, editing estimates, viewing schedules) work offline and sync when you reconnect. Payment processing and AI features require an internet connection.

How do I cancel my subscription?

From Settings → Subscription, you can cancel or change plans anytime. Your data stays accessible until the end of the current billing period.

I found a bug or have a feature request.

Please email help@mageid.app with a description and, if possible, a screenshot or video. We read every message.

Business Hours
