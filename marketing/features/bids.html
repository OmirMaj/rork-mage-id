Every federal opportunity, one tap away.

MAGE ID pulls active solicitations from SAM.gov (the official federal opportunities registry) and surfaces them in a feed built for mobile. No clunky government website, no lost bookmarks — just the opportunities that match what you build.

  • Live feed from SAM.gov opportunities API
  • Refreshed multiple times per day
  • Deep-link to full solicitation docs
  • Quick-save to your bid pipeline

Filters that actually match how you bid.

Dial in exactly what you want to see. Set-aside filters surface opportunities reserved for certifications you hold — 8(a), WOSB, HUBZone, SDVOSB. NAICS filters match your business codes. Location filters keep things regional.

  • NAICS-code and PSC filters
  • Set-aside filters: 8(a), WOSB, EDWOSB, HUBZone, SDVOSB, Total Small Business
  • State / ZIP / radius location filters
  • Dollar range filters (min + max estimated value)
  • Response deadline filters (next 7 / 14 / 30 days)

Save searches, get notified.

Found the right mix of filters? Save the search. New opportunities that match get pushed to your phone. No more refreshing SAM.gov every morning over coffee.

  • Unlimited saved searches
  • Push notifications when new matches appear
  • Daily digest email for saved searches
  • "Similar to this" recommendations
