Scheduling & Gantt

Pinch-to-zoom Gantt,
right on your phone.

Build schedules with phases, dependencies, and milestones — no laptop required. Critical path auto-calculates. When change orders add days, the schedule shifts automatically.

Phase-based task management

Organize work by phase — sitework, foundation, framing, finishes — with each task carrying a duration, start/end, dependencies, and progress percentage.

  • Drag to reschedule, tap to edit duration
  • Predecessor / successor dependencies with FS, SS, FF lag
  • Percent-complete tracking that rolls up to the phase
  • Milestone markers that stand out visually

Critical path, highlighted.

The app computes the critical path every time you edit a task — no plugin, no separate tool. Tasks on the critical path glow red so you always know what's driving your finish date.

  • CPM computed automatically from dependencies
  • Total float shown per task
  • Red highlighting for zero-float tasks
  • Change order schedule impact applies instantly

AI schedule risk forecast Coming · FY26

On the roadmap: every schedule scanned for tasks likely to slip — weather exposure, crew size, dependency chains, and historical performance — surfaced as high / medium / low risk flags with mitigation recommendations.

  • Severity scoring with clear reasoning
  • Completion confidence and predicted end date
  • Specific mitigation recommendations
  • Refresh on demand or scheduled
