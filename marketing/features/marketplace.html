Local supplier directory.

A curated directory of construction suppliers — lumber yards, steel, concrete, masonry, MEP — across the nine largest construction metros in the US. Updated weekly. Filter by material type, distance, and delivery options.

  • Indexed metros: NYC, LA, Chicago, Houston, Phoenix, Philadelphia, San Antonio, San Diego, Dallas
  • Filter by material category (lumber, concrete, steel, finishes, MEP, tools)
  • Distance-sorted with drive time estimates
  • Delivery availability and lead times
  • One-tap call, navigate, or open website

Live material pricing that matches your estimates.

Common construction materials priced by region, refreshed weekly. Use them to sanity-check an estimate, or pull them directly into line items when you're drafting a new project. No more "pricing from memory" from a 2022 job.

  • Weekly price refreshes by metro
  • Common-material catalog: lumber grades, rebar, concrete, drywall, pipe, conductor
  • Pull directly into estimate line items
  • Historical price chart per item (trend line)
  • Regional price variance flagged (when your zip is 20%+ above average)

Equipment rentals — bookable.

Excavator for Thursday? Scissor lift for next week? Find, compare, and book rental equipment from local yards without leaving the app. Rental cost flows into your project budget automatically.

  • Categories: earthmoving, lifts, compaction, pumps, generators, hand tools
  • Price comparison across local rental yards
  • Reserve and track rentals — pickup, return, billing
  • Rental cost auto-added to project's equipment line
  • Damage / deposit tracking
