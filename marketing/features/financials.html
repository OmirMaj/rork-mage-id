Open Book / GMP

The transparency homeowners actually want.

Flip a project into Open Book mode and the homeowner sees every dollar — budget, committed, actual, projected final cost — by phase. Run a GMP contract and they see real cost vs. the cap, with savings shared by the rules in your contract. The kind of trust feature enterprise PM software doesn't ship for residential because it's not who they're selling to.

  • Toggle Open Book or GMP on any project — fixed-price contracts stay private by default.
  • Per-phase breakdown: framing, electrical, finishes — each with budget vs. actual.
  • GMP cap surfaced with live "vs. cap" delta. Overruns flagged, savings highlighted.
  • Contractor fee (% or flat) shown transparently — builds trust the right way.
  • Auto-updates every time you sign a sub commitment or pay an invoice.
  • Render in the live portal — no separate spreadsheet to maintain.

Earned Value Management

PV, EV, AC, CPI, SPI — the full EVM panel.

Classic earned value metrics calculated continuously from your schedule and committed costs. Know whether you're ahead of schedule, under budget, or in trouble — with numbers that match what any PMP or federal PM expects to see.

  • Planned Value (PV) from the baseline schedule
  • Earned Value (EV) from % complete × budget
  • Actual Cost (AC) from committed + paid costs
  • Cost Performance Index (CPI) and Schedule Performance Index (SPI)
  • Estimate at Completion (EAC) and Variance at Completion (VAC)
  • Classic S-curve visualization (PV / EV / AC over time)
